We are very happy to be able to welcome you to a public lecture by the political theorist Wendy Brown!

Postliberalism:  A Prolegomenon for Reparative Democracy

Liberal democracy is in crisis, but such crises are always the scene for the emergence of new forms. While the Right has developed a substantive intellectual and political postliberalism, the Left tends to remain focused on critiques of capitalism and/or restoring liberal democracy, ignoring the task of developing democratic futures apart from those contoured by liberalism.  This talk argues for a postliberal democracy, one that has political economy, political ecology and a reparative rather than progressive orientation at its heart.  It is the prologue for a project on "reparative democracy."
