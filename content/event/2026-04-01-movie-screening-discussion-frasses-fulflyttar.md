Moving trucks roll through Malmö, Gothenburg and Norrköping. Catching the eye of many with political slogans and messages about supporting drunks and philosophers. A creative moving company with a great sense of humour and a big heart, which dares to go against the grain. Find out how the work collective and multi-taskers Frasses Fulflyttar work and hear their thoughts on work, society and solidarity.

After the film, there will be a discussion with Frassarna and filmmaker Ingrid Ekman. After the discussion, the usual Wednesday pub will open. Where there will be board games, food and drinks!

The film has English subtitles, the discussion is in Swedish.

- Cast: The Frasses in Frasses Fulflyttar and some customers
