✨ Join grassroots- and union organizers, movement builders, party organizers, programmers and software developers in Malmö, Sweden for a 3 day-event of workshops, panels, knowledge-sharing, dinners and party. Stay tuned for updates!

## 📅 When: 15-17 May 2026 (New date)
- 📍 Where: Malmö, Sweden (exact location tba)
- 🌟 What: We will gather some 250-300 organizers and developers from our international network for this first installment. After this, - the event will take place every two years to build a growing, learning community over time.
- 💌 Stay in touch: https://clarafoundation.se/organize-movement-meetup.../

## 👥 WHO IS IT FOR?
If any of the following applies, Organize! Movement Meetup is for you:
- You want to be inspired, meet new people in the leftist movement and have fun.
- You are an organizer who wants to learn more about organizing and build your network.
- You are a programmer or designer interested in organizing for a cause.
- You want to share the knowledge you already have about organizing or programming in a community of likeminded people.

## 📝 WHAT IS IT ABOUT?
In workshops, panel discussions, one-to-ones you will be:
- Learning best practices as an organizer: how to work with door-knocking, phone banking, or recruiting new members.
- Sharing strategies for growing in power.
- Learning about strategic use of language, skills and tech.
- Building the relationships we need as a movement – with members and with each other.
- Making the most of the network while enjoying dinners – and a huge party.
