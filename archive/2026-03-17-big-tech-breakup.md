Sick of USA Big Tech? Lets divest from it together!

This is not a talk. This is a do-it-together session.

We keep procrastinating on moving our stuff. So now we want to get together for a chill evening to socialise and move our stuff off USA Big Tech platforms to more ethical alternatives.

We can use https://di.day/de/mehr-alternativen as a starting point. Their focus is on digital sovereignty. Of course, we can chat about whether our needs might be different based on different ethics and concerns.

Email, cloud storage, messaging, social media... Let's reclaim our lives, one app at a time. 

Bring your devices, questions, AND friends!

Expect:

✨ No tech expertise required

✨ Relaxed atmosphere

✨ Help from the community

✨ Resources and recommendations

✨ Chat about digital sovereignty and platform ethics

We don't have all the answers but we'll figure it out together. Everyone's welcome!
